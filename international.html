International Students

Welcome to STSC University — your global gateway to quality education in Ghana.

Global Community

Study in Ghana

STSC University welcomes students from across Africa and the world. With international directorates in Liberia, Nigeria, Switzerland, and India, we are truly a global institution.

🌎 Why Ghana?

  • English-speaking country
  • Stable democracy & peaceful environment
  • Affordable cost of living vs. other African nations
  • Rich cultural heritage & warm hospitality
  • ECOWAS mobility for West African nationals

🎓 Why STSC?

  • Accredited 4-year bachelor's degrees
  • Hands-on, career-focused curriculum
  • International directorates near you
  • Affordable tuition with scholarship options
  • Supportive international student community

💳 Fees (International)

  • Engineering: $2,200/year
  • Computing: $1,900/year
  • Business: $1,700/year
  • BA English: $1,500/year
  • Accommodation: $400-650/year
Directorates

Our International Directorates

STSC University has established international directorates to serve students across the globe. Contact the directorate nearest to you.

🇪🇪 Liberia

Our Liberia directorate serves West African students with recruitment, admissions support, and academic counselling. Contact us for guidance on applying from Liberia, Sierra Leone, or The Gambia.

🇲🇽 Nigeria

Our Nigeria directorate provides in-country support for Nigerian applicants, including application assistance, document verification, and pre-departure orientation.

🇨🇳 Switzerland

Our Switzerland directorate supports European and international students with admissions, visa guidance, and academic partnerships.

🇮🇳 India

Our India directorate assists South Asian students with the application process, credential evaluation, and cultural integration support.

Support

International Student Support

We provide comprehensive support services to help international students succeed at STSC University.

📞 Visa Assistance

Guidance on student visa applications, document requirements, and interview preparation for Ghana Immigration Service.

🏢 Accommodation

On-campus hostel accommodation with 24/7 security, meal plans, and dedicated international student housing options.

📚 Academic Support

English language support, academic advising, tutoring services, and orientation programme for all new international students.
