Requirements

Entry Requirements

STSC University welcomes applicants from diverse educational backgrounds. Below are the standard entry pathways.

🎓 WASSCE Applicants

  • Credit (A1–C6) in English Language
  • Credit (A1–C6) in Mathematics (Core)
  • Credit (A1–C6) in three relevant electives
  • Aggregate score of 24 or better
  • Applicable to all programmes

🎓 SSSCE Applicants

  • Credit (A–D) in English Language
  • Credit (A–D) in Mathematics (Core)
  • Credit (A–D) in three relevant electives
  • Aggregate score of 24 or better
  • Passing year: 2006 or later

🎓 Mature / Diploma Entry

  • Mature: 25 years or older
  • Proven work experience (2+ years)
  • Pass STSC entrance exam & interview
  • Diploma: HND or equivalent
  • Eligible for Level 200 placement

Financial Aid

Scholarships & Financial Aid

We believe finances should never be a barrier to quality education. STSC University offers a range of financial support options.

★ Merit Scholarship

Awarded to students with exceptional WASSCE/SSSCE results (Aggregate 12 or better). Covers up to 40% of tuition.

💰 Need-Based Aid

Financial assistance for students from low-income households. Covers 20–30% of tuition. Requires supporting documents.

📈 Early Bird Discount

Apply before the early deadline and receive a 10% discount on first-year tuition. Open to all applicants.
