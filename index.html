About Us

Excellence in Higher Education

STSC University commenced with 14 students on 23rd August 2013, born out of a passion to raise unquestionable leaders with academic excellence and ethical integrity.

We offer 12+ accredited bachelor's degree programmes across Engineering, Technology, Business, and the Humanities — each designed to meet national standards while preparing students for global opportunities.

  • Accredited by Ghana Tertiary Education Commission
  • Industry-aligned curriculum
  • Modern labs & workshops
  • Work-integrated learning

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about studying at STSC University.

Complete the online application form on this page or visit our admissions page. Submit your WASSCE/SSSCE results, personal details, and programme preference. Our admissions team will respond within 48 hours.
WASSCE: Credit (A1–C6) in English, Mathematics, and three relevant subjects. SSSCE: Credit (A–D) in similar subjects. Mature entry (25+ years) and diploma holders may also qualify.
All our bachelor's degree programmes are 4 years (8 semesters) full-time, following the Ghanaian university standard. Some programmes offer part-time options spanning 5–6 years.
Yes. We offer merit-based scholarships and need-based financial aid. Early applicants receive priority consideration. Contact our admissions team for a personalised breakdown.
Yes. STSC University is fully accredited by the Ghana Tertiary Education Commission (GTEC). All our programmes meet national academic standards.
