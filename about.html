Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence

STSC University commenced with 14 students on 23rd August 2013, born out of a passion to raise unquestionable leaders who would transform their communities and the world.

Since then, we have grown steadily into a multi-disciplinary university offering accredited bachelor's degree programmes at our Kasoa-Nyanyano campus and through our international directorates in Liberia, Nigeria, Switzerland, and India.

Our commitment to raising God-fearing, ethical, and professionally competent graduates remains the driving force behind everything we do.

Mission & Vision

Our Purpose

Guided by a commitment to academic excellence and national development.

🎓 Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality tertiary education that equips students with the knowledge, practical skills, and ethical values needed to excel in their chosen fields and contribute meaningfully to Ghana's socio-economic development.

🛰 Our Vision

To be a leading private university in West Africa, recognised for academic excellence, innovation, and producing graduates who are globally competitive and locally relevant.

Our Journey

STSC Timeline

From humble beginnings to a multi-disciplinary university — key milestones since our founding.

23 August 2013
STSC University founded with 14 students, born out of a passion to raise unquestionable leaders.
2015
Expanded to 6 degree programmes; moved to permanent Kasoa-Nyanyano campus.
2018
First graduating class of 48 students. Established international directorate in Liberia.
2021
Launched Engineering and Computing programmes. Opened Nigeria and Switzerland directorates.
2024
Expanded to 11+ programmes. India directorate established. 2,400+ alumni worldwide.
2026
STSC University continues to grow — raising leaders who transform communities across Ghana and the world.
